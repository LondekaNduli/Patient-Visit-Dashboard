## Patient-Visit-Dashboard
The Patient Visit Analytics Dashboard was developed to monitor clinic activity, patient demographics, appointment outcomes, and departmental performance.
The dashboard provides healthcare stakeholders with actionable insights into patient visits and operational efficiency.

# Key Findings
# 1. Clinic Activity Overview
The clinic recorded 1,500 total visits.
These visits were generated by 465 unique patients.
The average patient age was 46.17 years.
The average waiting time was 91.52 minutes.

Insight: The clinic serves a recurring patient base, with patients visiting multiple times throughout the year.

# 2. Monthly Visit Trends
Patient visits fluctuated throughout the year.
October recorded the highest number of visits.
July experienced the lowest patient volume.
Visit volumes generally remained stable between 120–140 visits per month.

Business Impact: Staffing and appointment scheduling can be adjusted during peak months to improve service delivery and reduce waiting times.

# 3. Department Performance

Department ranking by total visits:

General Practice (~500 visits)
Pediatrics
Dermatology
Unknown
Cardiology

Insight: General Practice is the clinic's primary service area and accounts for a significant share of patient traffic.
Recommendation: Consider allocating additional resources and appointment slots to General Practice to manage demand efficiently.

# 4. Appointment Outcomes

Appointment distribution:

Completed: 513 visits (34.2%)
No Show: 508 visits (33.9%)
Cancelled: 254 visits (16.9%)
Unknown: 225 visits (15.0%)

Insight: The No Show rate is nearly equal to the number of completed appointments.
Business Concern: High no-show rates can reduce clinic efficiency, create scheduling gaps, and negatively impact patient access.
Recommendation: Implement appointment reminders via SMS, email, or phone calls to improve attendance rates.

# 5. Data Quality Findings

During data preparation:

Duplicate records were removed.
Missing age values were handled using median imputation.
Gender values were standardized.
Department names were standardized.
Appointment status values were cleaned and normalized.

Insight: Data cleaning improved reporting accuracy and ensured reliable dashboard metrics.
